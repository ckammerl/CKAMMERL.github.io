03/15/14 - Some good designed websites

  1. Cut Magazine
  2. Amazon local
  3. One Medical Group

Checkout the three websites listed above. These are some of my favorite sites in terms of design. They all address different audiences and have different target groups and purposes.

Cut Magazine addresses the Do-it-Yourself community and delves into every aspect of DYI - especially into fashion. Amazon local is a shopping platform dedicated to advertise special offerings in your local area across almost every category (entertainment, health/wellness, sport, travel etc.). And last but not least One Medical Group is a healthcare group established to improve access to physicians in the Bay Area.

Despite their different purposes, all three pages and platforms have their simple and clean design in common. None of them is overloaded with content or distracting, oversized images and/or moving elements; key information is highlighted (color and/or font-size) and navigating through their pages and accessing the individual content is quiet easy.

They are well structured and allow you to get the required information within 1-2 clicks. I am not saying these three web pages are perfect; there is room for improvement. Looking at the Cut Magazine's blog for instance, I would recommend adding additional hyperlinks and enlarging the blog posts' font-size to improve readability.
