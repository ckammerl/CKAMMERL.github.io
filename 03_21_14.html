Pick one and write a couple of sentences about why one of the values you chose is important to you.

I find it difficult to pick just one of the values above and to explain why it's important to me. I believe that they are heavily related and appear as a mix when I'm happy and satisfied. But to break it down, I think one of the most important values is free time. This might now look like I am a lazy person but for me, free time is something that helps me reach my goals - at work and in private live.

I would say having a couple of minutes/ hours just for me, is my greatest value because it's me deciding what to do and when and with whome. At work, only 5 minutes of freetime is the option to step back and to free yourself when you're stuck. Free time helps me to achieve the states that are bound to the other values I highlighted. Like having time to be adventurous, to be creative, to be reflective and credible, to be with my closest friends and family, to listen to music etc.
